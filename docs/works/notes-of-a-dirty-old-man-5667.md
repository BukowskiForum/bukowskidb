## Excerpt
!!! quote "Notes of a Dirty Old Man"
    There wasn't any food around but I did have 2 or 3 classical record albums. I had found a place in town where I could exchange 3 record albums for 2. With a minimum of purchases—at those rare times when I did have money—I was able to listen to many albums until I tired of them. It wasn't long before I had each note memorized...
